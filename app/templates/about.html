The Challenge We're Solving

Farmers in Tier 2/3 India face critical challenges that limit their income potential and sustainability.

Market Opacity

Excessive middlemen create non-transparent pricing, leaving farmers with minimal returns despite fair market values.

High Wastage

Poor logistics and post-harvest management result in 15-25% post-harvest losses for perishable goods.

Uncertainty & Risk

Farmers lack predictive data on weather and market demand, making cultivation planning a high-risk endeavor.

Our Three-Layered Intelligent Solution

Built from the ground up for Tier 2/3 India's unique agricultural ecosystem

Core Marketplace

Direct B2B Linkage

Multilingual (vernacular) app connecting farmers directly to nearby retailers, bulk buyers, and processors within 50-100 km radius.

  • Low-Bandwidth Design: Visual and voice-command interface for feature phones
  • Real-time Connections: Instant farmer-to-buyer matching
  • Transparent Pricing: No hidden middleman margins

Predictive Intelligence

AI-Powered Decision Making

Our advanced machine learning models transform raw agricultural data into actionable insights for farmers.

  • Price Forecasting: Predict optimal selling prices 3-7 days in advance
  • Satellite-Backed Advisory: Detect crop stress and pest outbreaks early
  • Weather Alerts: Hyper-local weather data for better planning

Automated Logistics

Smart Supply Chain

Upon successful sale, our system automatically matches consignments with verified local transport owners.

  • Seamless Integration: Automatic transport matching after sale
  • Transparent Charges: Fair logistics pricing based on distance
  • Loss Reduction: Optimized routes minimize post-harvest losses
